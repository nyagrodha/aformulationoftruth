In the limitless world of poetry, the poet alone is the lord of creation.
Just as he enjoys the world so, too, he transforms it.
For every reader finds himself. The writer's work is merely a kind of
optical instrument that makes it possible for the reader to discern
what, without this piece, he would, perhaps, never have seen in himself.
