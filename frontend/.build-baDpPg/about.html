form itself is emptiness — emptiness alone is form

Working in a hard-hearts-not-necessary zone this project bears imprint the impossibility I discover as a crackhead attempting to project aunified self coherent on any given day. Since in this life alone I seems to be handily six to seven versions itself. I-I witness this slow marinating in flesh one wrestle with being a person— to act as one ought, to live up to the categorical imperative. Yet what is it to be a self through time? Having risked saying something thoughtless, we may not want to identify a self as unified or coherent in any singular person. How do I become such different persons in life, yet am still called by the same name? Who is this self, this I am? I call myself a self‑inquisitive intellect operator working in a hard‑hearts‑not‑necessary zone. Righteousness, embodiment, and imagination intertwine: the bonafide tool belt of building, the staircase in abstract space, the democratic tongue that gives voice.
