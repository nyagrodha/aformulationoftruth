welcome to a formulation of truth

a creation of a self-inquisitive intellect operator

working in a hard-hearts not necessary zone

to be a person, to act as one oughta live up to the
categorical imperative, in other words, to be
cohesive as one self through time (having said something thoughtless)
one may not want to identify a 'self' as unified in any 'person'


truth resides in the reconstruction of events without precedent
in a world where nothing repeats itself exactly; where sense
