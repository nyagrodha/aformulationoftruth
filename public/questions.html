The Proust Questionnaire

35 Introspective Questions

Originating in 17th or 18th c. Victorian social circles, Marcel Proust popularized this parlor game of yore in the 20th c. These questions orient one's gaze inward toward the self. Respondents thereby share a morsel or two of what lies to be discovered beneath the surface of social graces & decorum. Come to think of it, it's a shame there isn't a question about penis size.
