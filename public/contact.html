‚öñÔ∏è What This Means For You

Practically speaking: Finnish hosting means your data could theoretically be subject to Finnish law enforcement requests with proper judicial process. Finland is a well-functioning democracy with strong rule of law‚Äîauthorities cannot simply demand data without court oversight. However, Finland's intelligence cooperation and EU membership mean there are more pathways for international law enforcement access than in Iceland.

Our mitigation: We minimize what we collect and encrypt what we must store. If served with a valid Finnish court order, we could only provide encrypted data and metadata we don't retain (we've disabled access logging). We cannot provide what we don't have.

Why Finland then? Cost, latency, and infrastructure quality. Flokinet's Finnish servers offer excellent connectivity to both European and Asian users at a price point that keeps this project sustainable. We believe transparency about jurisdiction trade-offs serves you better than marketing a privacy jurisdiction we don't actually use.
