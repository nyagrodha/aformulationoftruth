what is a formulation of truth?

May be it's a bellwether the declining currency of honesty that aformulationoftruth.com still existed to register as a domain. Many folks make a lot of claims in the name of truth. I make no claim with regard to this questionnaire and its ability to discover something essential within or about us. The phrase 'a formulation of truth' was arrived at during a graduate seminar I attended as a student of Sanskrit literature. The professor, JB, and the class translated brahmā, the nominative singular (i.e. I) form of brahman. This undeclined word 'brahman' does not exist but theoretically in Sanskrit insofar as it would not/coul a neuter noun -an ending noun in Sanskrit, as 'a formulation of truth'.

vividhā racanā: variegated composition

This website serves the Proust questionnaire dba karuppacāmi keḷvittaḷ. For a number of reasons I chose the name karuppacāmi: because it translates into ஆங்கலம் (āngalaṁ) as something like 'dark lord' and darkness appeals to me; to appeal to Tamil culture; out of devotion to bhairava, and as comic relief in memorium a கோழிக்கஞ்ச (Kōḷikkuñcu) I raised on handground pulses the summer of 2013.

Howsoever distinct the dark protector of boundaries from the incarnation of śiva as the horror of time, Kāla bhairava, for time consumes everything, I have cultivated a sense of self within the Sanskrit language for over half a lifetime. Being a perpetual outsider to a culture and way of life infinitely more appealing to the heartmind than this culture into which I was born and the kuladeva, or family god I know, my take is more irreverent at times. And intentionally so the mixing of reverence antonym with heady truths concerning the nature of reality.
