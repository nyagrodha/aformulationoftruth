X25519 Encryption Setup

Paid users can encrypt their questionnaire responses with X25519 encryption.

How it works:

  • Keys are generated securely on your device
  • Your public key is stored on our server for encryption
  • Questionnaire responses can be encrypted before sending
  • You can share encrypted responses via shareable links
Your keys will be managed securely. Contact support if you need to reset them.
