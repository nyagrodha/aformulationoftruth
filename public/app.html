The poet's mind is a forest of delights:
a dwelling place for bliss.
Here, sin is a vehicle to holiness
Here, suffering turns into a joyful chant
Here, peace becomes a journey toward immortality.
truth resides in the reconstruction
of events without precedent

in a world where nothing
repeats itself exactly;

where one's sense
comes from the association of memory.

देवदेवप्रभावकसमम्
कस्ते बोधुं प्रभवति परं देवदेव प्रकाम्
समस्तं विश्वरूपं च धृतं च त्वया ।
भक्त्या केवलमात्मना तवांहं अनाथः
स्तोतुं चेष्टमि अहहहे सःसः मे सहसं ॥ २ ॥

Who is able to know the gravity of your being from whom this variegated creation springs into existence? You can be understood through devotion alone. I wish, therefore, to propitiate you; forgive me for this audacious endeavour.
